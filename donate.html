ADOPT-A-BEACH™

Thousands of volunteers -- families, schools, businesses and community groups -- participate in the Alliance's Adopt-a-Beach™ Program. Volunteer to adopt a beach year round, or for just one day in September!

We’ll keep you up to date on your donation’s impact by sending you:

  • Our quarterly print publication, WaterMarks, including the Adopt-a-Beach™ and Annual Report special editions.
  • A monthly e-newsletter about Great Lakes issues, events and volunteer opportunities throughout the region.
  • Action Alerts on timely issues where your voice can make a difference.
