Our Programs

Our vision is that, by working with you, we can conserve and restore our Great Lakes for generations of people and wildlife.

Clean Water

The Alliance's Water Quality Program is dedicated to clean water that protects the health of the people, fish and wildlife of the Great Lakes.

Conserving Water

Some 40 million people rely on the Great Lakes for drinking water. As the pressure on fresh water mounts around the world, the Alliance is working to ensure that these waters are maintained and protected.

Invasive Species

The Alliance is working to permanently stop the devastating Asian carp and other invasives from entering the Great Lakes by restoring the natural physical barriers that once separated Lake Michigan and the rest of the Great Lakes

Coastal Conservation

Working to restore important coastal habitats that feed and nourish the Great Lakes by bringing together multi-jurisdictional stakeholders to restore ailing coastal habitat -- the lakes' first line of defense against pollution and a safe harbor for fish and wildlife, no matter where it is.

Great Lakes, Great education

The Alliance for the Great Lakes is dedicated to fostering an ethic of learning, appreciation and care for the Great Lakes.
